Meet Madlayn

I have been a hairstylist for more than 20 years. I'm very passionate about what I do and I enjoy meeting new people. I've always had a desire to open a men's hair salon on campus that could accommodate students and staff with affordable prices.

In 2012, PizZazz opened its doors — and has been providing a welcoming environment for people of all nationalities ever since. When you sit in my chair, you're not just getting a haircut. You're getting a conversation, a real opinion, and a cut that'll turn heads.

— Madlayn
